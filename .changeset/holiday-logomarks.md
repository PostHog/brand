`Logo.Logomark` gains a `holiday` prop to dress the hedgehog up for a season — `"christmas"` (a Santa hat, the same one the PostHog app wears in December) or `"halloween"` (a witch hat). The accessory rides the head, so it keeps its festive colors in every variant (including `mono`) and jumps along when the mark jumps. Nothing is date-driven — the consumer decides when it's the season.
